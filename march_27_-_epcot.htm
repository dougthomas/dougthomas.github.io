March 28 - Kelly's

This was to be our last day doin' the Disney thing.  We had not seen much of EPCOT (Experimental Prototype Community Of Tomorrow, according to Jerry Mander), and we really like it, so we wanted to finish it off.  We spent some of the morning riding the rides we were interested in, but wanted to spend the majority of our time looking at 'the countries'.  Below, Jody and Jordan check out the Fez' in Morocco. 

Japan was also a favorite.  Below, Jordan models some silk Pajamas that she briefly considered purchasing.

We revisited Morocco in time to see a troupe of acrobats performing.  Below, Joya caught this very-well-taken image of one of the performers mid-way through a flip.

Late that afternoon, Robb and Meagan rendezvoused with us.  We talked a lot and wandered among the countries or listened to some of the live music.  We all especially enjoyed an Irish group called 'Barrage'.  They are a world-music fusion band with 7 fiddlers, 2 guitarists and 2 percussionist.   We liked them so much that we even bought a CD at Disney prices.

As you can see from the below, Joya and Jordan really hit it off with Megan, and vise-versa.

That night's dinner reservations were at Alfredo's, in EPCOT Italy.  Jody and Doug had planned to treat Robb and Megan, but Robb beat us to the punch and the tab, and held firm, so we graciously accepted the hospitality and meal -- both were great.  It was a wonderful end to both our Disney and Florida trip segments.
