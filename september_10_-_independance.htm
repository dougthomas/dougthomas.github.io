We drove West from Mansfield to a lovely state park near Independence, Kansas, set into a winding canyon.  Our campsite overlooked the lake -- and the pit toilets, which you can see silhouetted in front of the lake. 

The next  morning we drove to Independence.  This was another "Little House" location -- the Little House on the Prairie was here -- or rather its reconstruction was.  It was not far out of our way, so we traveled the back roads out to see it, only to find that it was closed.  However, we were able to snap a picture from the street.  You can see why she called it a little house -- this one room log cabin was home to the family of 5, plus their dog Jack.

After a quick visit, we continued our Westward rush, this time across most of Kansas.
